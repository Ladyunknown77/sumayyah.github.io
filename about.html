ABOUT OUR CAUSE

Why This Matters to Me

Hi. My name is Sumayyah Hatun. I created this website because, as a Fijian, I've seen firsthand how vulnerable my home country and other small island nations are to the devastating effects of climate change. It's incredibly frustrating to witness our communities suffer disproportionately from a crisis largely fueled by developed nations and large corporations.

I care deeply about ensuring a promising and livable future for all Fijians, where humans and nature can thrive alongside each other. This project is my way of raising awareness and advocating for urgent, equitable global climate action. We need to hold major emitters accountable and secure the support necessary for our islands to adapt and build resilience.
