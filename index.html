Urgent, equitable global climate action is essential to protect the homes and futures of vulnerable island nations like Fiji.

Fiji on the Front Lines of Climate Change

Fiji and other small island nations are facing severe and escalating threats from climate change impacts—such as sea-level rise and extreme weather—despite being minimal contributors to global emissions, largely due to historical industrialization and ongoing high emissions from developed nations and large corporations.
